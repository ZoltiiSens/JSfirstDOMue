How to play?

In the left bottom side of the playing area enter the number(between 1 and 10) you think is guessed

Click "Choose" button and look at the right side.

If you guessed right you will understand it. If not, you would have a prompt - was this number bigger or smaller than guessed

Look at your score - each wrong step will decrease the score by one. Higher score - better result!

Good luck!
